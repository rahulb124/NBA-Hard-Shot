An Open Website addressing the FBI, Arguing for the Protection of Individuals Data from Government Agencies

As you, The Federal Bureau of Investigation, should know, after the San Bernadino terrorist attack in December of 2015, the FBI wanted help from Apple Inc. to "unlock" the iPhone of one of the terrorists from the attack. Apple, however opposed this and declined to help. As a result, you, the goverment agencies, got the Justice Department to order Apple to unlock the security on the phone, in result creating massive legal complications. Following Apple declining to unlock the phone, you hired professional hackers on a one time basis to "hack" the security features on the phone.

Myself and many others, do not agree with the FBI's demand that companies such as Apple should be required to risk the personal data of its customers, as well as from the position of a customer I would not want my own personal data to be decrypted for any reason.
