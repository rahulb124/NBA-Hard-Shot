Consumer's Perspective on Privacy

Along with many tech companies, the consumers of these companies have a high disdain for the FBI's requests.

From the perspective of a customer of these companies, the FBI's request is not accepted well, because of the implications it has on the general public. If companies were forced to create a Backdoor for the FBI, customers would often times lose respect for the company, and in some cases find a new company to purchase products from.

Consumers would be greatly affected by a backdoor as it reduced the security of their personal data. Individuals put a high importance on their encrypted data or else they wouldn't be creating passwords and storing their data securely. With the introduction of a backdoor, it reduces an individual's private safety and security as the Backdoor introduces a "backdoor" for hackers as well.
