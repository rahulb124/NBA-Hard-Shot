Tech Companies Persepective on Privacy

There are many tech companies that are in full support of Apple's decision to reject the FBI's demand for a Backdoor to it's customers products.

The first reason tech companies are not in support of the FBI's decision is, because what you are asking of the tech companies in terms of a backdoor does not exist and can not just be created as a one time thing. Apple has stated that its encryption and security is secure that they could not even decrypt the devices if they wanted to. Similarly, if companies such as apple do create a backdoor for the FBI, it leaves their products more vulnerable to hackers. The strong encryption and security developed by its engineers are undermined due to the backdoor for the FBI.

Along with this, if tech companies give in to orders from the FBI, its customer will not respect these companies as much as they are sacrificing the consumers security. Tech companies who are forced to fall to the FBI's demands not only lose reputation, but can also lose customers to companies who are not asked by the FBI for a backdoor.
