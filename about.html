About: NBA Hard Shot

What is this?

This webapp uses a python backend to scrape stats.nba.com to acquire the raw data for shots of the top 2016-2017 all stars in the NBA. The backend then sends the front end the data that is important and the frontend shows the analysis of the data. The main focus of this webapp is to determine which NBA All-star is able to make the most difficult shots(eg:Low time on shotclock, tight defender defense, dribbles, and touch time) the most consistently and the most often.

What can I do on the webapp?

You can look at the Radar chart for each player to see which difficult factor each player excels at while compared to the average of All-stars. You can sort a table by each factor and overall total to see which player ranks where. And finally you can compare the radar charts for 2 specific players.



All data comes from the current season (2016-2017).
